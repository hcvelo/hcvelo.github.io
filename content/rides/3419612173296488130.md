## CAFT Santa Dash 

30 miles
CAFT Santa Dash
Register at:
https://register.enthuse.com/ps/event/CAFTSantaDash2025

All the cycling routes will pass The Children’s Adventure Farm Trust in Millington where participants will be able to warm up with a hot beverage (hot chocolate or mulled wine) and mince pie, before putting on the Santa costume for the last leg of the journey back to Altrincham. Walking Santas will receive refreshments at the start.
On return, Santas will be welcomed by the “REAL” man in red who will award them with a finisher medal before a hearty lunch (hot pot with all the trimmings &amp; hot dogs).
 
Provisional timings are 9am for 60-mile ride, 10.30am for 30-miles ride, 11am for Family ride; 10am for 8-mile walk, 11.15am for 4-mile walk.  Routes and exact timings will be confirmed nearer to the time.
All participants will receive Santa Suit, Finisher Medal, Refreshments (mince pie / hot chocolate or mulled wine), and hearty lunch.
 
Entry is £20 for an adult / £12 u18.
 
You can register using the following link: CAFT Santa Dash 2025 | The Children&#39;s Adventure Farm Trust
