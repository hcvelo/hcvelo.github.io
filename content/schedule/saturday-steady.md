## Saturday Steady

A social "no drop" ride at a steady pace. Usually flat-ish and with a coffee stop.

## When

Every Saturday, 08:00am

## Start Location

George & Dragon, Holmes Chapel, 08:00am

## Distance

Typically 40-50km


## Pace

21-24kph (13-15mph)
