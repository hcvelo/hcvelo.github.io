## Sunday Hilly Ride

A social "no drop" ride taking in some climbs. Usually includes a cafe stop.

## When

Every Sunday, 08:00

## Start Location

George & Dragon, Holmes Chapel, 08:00am

## Distance

Typically 60-80km with 600-1000+m of elevation gain

## Pace

26-28kph (16-17mph)
